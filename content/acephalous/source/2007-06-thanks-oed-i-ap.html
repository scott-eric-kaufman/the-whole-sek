Thanks, OED, I Appreciate the Sympathy

While proofing one of the articles I'm finishing today, I checked up on "underscore"  in the OED.  What I found disturbs me:

1952 S. KAUFFMANN Philanderer (1954) iv. 58 He could feel the stupidity of what he was writing being underscored by the stupidity of what was being written in the rooms next door.

There are so many things in there to dispirit me, I barely know where to begin.
