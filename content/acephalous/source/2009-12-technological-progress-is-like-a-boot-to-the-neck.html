Technological progress is like a boot to the neck.

What would my dissertation have looked like were this a reality?  Something like this:
Jack London claimed that and do I really want to start a sentence with the name of the author crap I'm quote unquote typing all this so yes comma insert comma insert comma fuck it so Jack London claimed that and I'm going to have to delete all this shit meaning it certainly isn't saving me any time fuck I'm going to have to delete that too period period caps lock period stop you fucker fine no commas no periods we can write this like Gertrude I always loved that bit in everybody's autobiography and shit is this thing still typing why yes it is asshole and it'll continue to so long as you think stop thinking and how do you do a period in this thing? Hey look a question mark I made a question mark now how do I make a comma? Hey look another question mark I guess I understand questions better than answers and what are you trying to tell me you fucking machine?
That's simulated, of course, but devastatingly accurate nonetheless.
