Podcasts: Yea, Nay, or Conditionally?

There's a poll (prominently displayed) on the site now.   (Those who missed The First Ever Acephalous Podcast, you can view it here.)  I'd appreciate if those who saw it would weigh in.  It was a little more time-consuming than the average post, but as much fun as spend quality time with the wife (ducks).  I wouldn't mind doing it again.  They would remain within the realm of parody, i.e. interviews with The Giant Head of Glenn Reynolds (with his answers plucked from his podcasts).  Doesn't strike me as a terrible idea, but I'd like a show of hands before I invest much time in it.

(The poll does indeed make the third noxious blog phenomenon I've indulged in this past week.  I pray you can forgive me.)
