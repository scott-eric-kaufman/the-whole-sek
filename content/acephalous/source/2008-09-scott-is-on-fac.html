Scott is on Facebook.

  • Scott is bored.
  • Scott is snooping around Facebook looking for people he used to know.
  • Scott is surprised the award-winning Broadway composer he knows uses such salty language.
  • Scott is really just feigning surprise.
  • Scott is already regretting this post.
  • Scott is telling you this is the best songs ever.
  • Scott is wondering why you're not listening to it already.
  • Scott is willing to wait until you get back.
  • Scott is surprised how few people he knows in the Acephalous group.
  • Scott is really surprised this time.
  • Scott is wondering whether random people are joining his group.
  • Scott is waving at the lurkers.
  • Scott is considering adding that guy he always hated because twelve years of hatred is a bonding experience.
  • Scott is someone who may know himself according to Facebook.
  • Scott is horrified by the groups some of his friends have joined.
  • Scott is remembering how different Louisiana is with regard to race relations.
  • Scott is sad.
  • Scott is realizing where all those random people came from the other day.
  • Scott is thinking he was there when that picture was taken in 1995.
  • Scott is almost positive that the leg on the right belongs to him.
  • Scott is knowing this because there is a lightning bolt shaved down its side.
  • Scott is thinking that writing fake Facebook updates is a superior response to boredom than those he had in high school.
  • Scott is remembering he went on three dates with that woman before she was Hispanic.
  • Scott is not sure how that happened.
  • Scott is talking about her becoming Hispanic because he knows how scored those dates with her.
  • Scott is telling you he asked her out and she answered in the affirmative.
  • Scott is of the opinion that he always knew those people would go to Harvard because they are bastards.
  • Scott is remembering why all these familiar-looking women with whom his friends are friends have the same first name as women he used to know.
  • Scott is remembering why he didn't go to Harvard.
  • Scott is not that bright.
  • Scott is however the only person from his graduating class to have earned a doctorate.
  • Scott is an elitist prig. 
  • Scott is not accidentally typing "prig" when he means "pig."
  • Scott is proving "prig" is a word by pointing at its entry in the OED.
  • Scott is doing this despite having been alone in the room the whole time.
  • Scott is changing the subject.
  • Scott is in love with Johan.
  • Scott is debating whether it would be bad form to add an old acquaintance solely to see how extensive the plastic surgery was.
  • Scott is aware even having this debate means he's a bad person.
  • Scott is always having known that guy was gay.
  • Scott is still hating that guy all these years later.
  • Scott is talking about a different guy there not the one he always knew was gay.
  • Scott is thinking that that seventh child qualifies as overkill.
  • Scott is thinking that the sixth one might have been too.
  • Scott is editing his Facebook page so when his students find it they'll mistakenly believe he's a professional.
  • Scott is aware he could keep this up forever.
  • Scott is hating Facebook.
