      Dear Cowardly Anonymous Commenters,

      [ 139 ] March 23, 2012 | SEK

      Because WordPress makes it incredibly difficult to ban you — and because you’re so delightfully stupid it’s often difficult to resist the urge to mock you with gleeful abandon — we would like you to know that there are banners on the right sidebar called “advertisements,” meaning that every time you fail to annoy us with one of your childish tantrums we make money.

      We repeat: when you mistakenly believe you’re scoring points against the upstanding citizens of our Liberal Death Cult, we make money. The more often you repeat your nonsense, the more money we make. So the next time you feel the urge to derail a thread with irrelevant comments about matters outside our areas of expertise, remember that each and every one of your flaccid barbs puts more money in our pockets.

      Thank you and have a nice day.
