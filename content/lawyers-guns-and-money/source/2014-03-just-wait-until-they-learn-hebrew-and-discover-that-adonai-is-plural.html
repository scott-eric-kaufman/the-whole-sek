        • Bloix says:
          March 28, 2014 at 5:43 pm

          Although “Elohim” has the masculine plural ending “im,” in the Bible it takes singular verbs and adjectives.

          Adonai is the possessive plural of adon “lord, master” (so “my lords”) but it also takes singular verbs and adjectives. (The singular possessive is “adoni,” which is what they would use if they dubbed Downton Abby into modern Hebrew.)

          So it’s not quite right to say they are plurals. They are in plural form, but grammatically they are treated as if they were singular.
